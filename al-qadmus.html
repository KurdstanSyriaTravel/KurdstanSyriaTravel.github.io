About Historic Al-Qadmus City

Al-Qadmus is proudly perched on high mountain peaks at altitudes exceeding 1,000 meters above sea level.

  • الجسر المعلق: يربط بين قمتين جبليتين.

Al-Atlal Zip Line (Al-Semaihiqa)

  • Sky Bike: The world's longest and highest suspended bicycles.
  • Suspended Bridge: Connecting two mountain peaks.
